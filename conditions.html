The scholarship eligibility criteria are as follows:

  • If 12th marks are ≥ 90% and 10th marks are ≥ 90%, you will receive a 50% scholarship.
  • If 12th marks are between 80% and 89% and 10th marks are ≥ 90%, you will receive a 25% scholarship.
  • If either 10th marks or 12th marks are < 80%, no scholarship will be provided.

Please note that the decision for granting scholarships is final and binding as per the institution's policy.

For any further inquiries, contact our support team at scholarships@institution.com.
