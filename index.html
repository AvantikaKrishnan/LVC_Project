About

A virtual class is an online learning environment where students and teachers connect in real-time through video conferencing and other digital tools.
It replicates a traditional classroom, allowing for interaction, discussions, and access to educational materials from any location. Virtual classes provide flexibility and convenience, making learning accessible to a wider audience.
